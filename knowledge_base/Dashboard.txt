# Dashboard Module Overview
The Photon Logistics Dashboard is an analytics module designed to monitor and analyze shipment operations and logistics performance.

It provides insights into:
- Shipment volume trends
- Shipment cost distribution
- Carrier partner performance
- Service provider activity

The dashboard enables users to make data-driven decisions by offering real-time and historical shipment analytics.

# Purpose of Dashboard

The primary purpose of the dashboard is to:

Provide operational visibility into shipment activities
Track shipment performance across providers
Analyze cost distribution among carriers
Monitor weekly shipment trends

# Service Provider Filters
Available Providers:
- All → Shows combined data
- AV Labs → Platform owner
- Client → External businesses
- Freight Forwarder 1 → Logistics partner
- Freight Forwarder 2 → Logistics partner
- Freight Forwarder 3 → Logistics partner

# Service Provider Definitions

## AV Labs
The primary organization operating the logistics platform
Owns and manages the Photon system own Rates

## Client
External companies using logistics services Rates
Examples: Vimla, Meditech

## Freight Forwarder 1
Third-party logistics provider
Handles shipment transportation and Rates

## Freight Forwarder 2
Another logistics partner
Supports shipment delivery operations and Rates

## Freight Forwarder 3
Additional logistics partner
Works alongside other forwarders and Rates

# Dashboard Analytics Components

The dashboard contains 4 main visual analytics sections:

## Last 4 Week Shipment

Description:
Displays shipment count over the past 4 weeks
Shows trends across different service providers and carriers

Purpose:
- Identify growth or decline in shipments
- Compare carrier performance

Chart Type: Bar Chart

## Current Week Shipment
Description:
- Displays shipment count for the current week
- Grouped by date and carrier

Purpose:
- Monitor daily shipment activity
- Track real-time performance

Chart Type: Bar Chart

## Last 4 Week Shipment Amount (INR)

Description:
- Shows total shipment cost distribution over the last 4 weeks
- Displays contribution of each carrier

Purpose:
- Analyze revenue/cost contribution
- Identify top-performing carriers

Chart Type: Pie Chart

## Current Week Shipment Amount (INR)

Description:
Displays shipment cost distribution for the current week

Purpose:
- Understand current financial distribution
- Identify dominant carrier

Chart Type: Pie Chart

# Chart Types Used
Bar Chart

Used for:
- Comparing shipment counts
- Visualizing trends over time
- Comparing providers or carriers

Pie Chart

Used for:
- Showing percentage distribution
- Identifying major contributors
- Understanding cost share

# Carriers (Couriers)
Carriers are logistics companies responsible for shipment delivery.

Common Carriers:
- Delhivery
- DHL
- FedEx
- Aramex
- UPS
- DTDC

# Carrier Selection Logic

Shipments are assigned to carriers based on:

- Route availability
- Cost efficiency
- Client preference
- Service level agreements (SLA)
